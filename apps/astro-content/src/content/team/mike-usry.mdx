Mike Usry is the founder and CEO of Southland Organics, a company dedicated to sustainable agriculture solutions. With a deep passion for soil health and animal welfare, Mike has spent years developing organic products that help farmers work with nature rather than against it.

## Background

Mike's journey into sustainable agriculture began with his discovery of humate deposits and their remarkable ability to improve soil health. This led him to found Southland Organics, where he has developed a range of products for poultry, turf, and general agriculture.

## Philosophy

At the core of Mike's approach is a simple belief: healthy soil creates healthy plants, healthy animals, and ultimately, healthier people. This philosophy drives every product and decision at Southland Organics.

## The Podcast

Mike hosts the Ag & Culture Podcast, where he shares conversations about what actually works in agriculture. From soil biology to poultry health, the podcast digs into the science and stories that matter to modern farmers and agricultural enthusiasts.
