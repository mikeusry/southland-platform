Isabella Dobbins served as Content Manager at Southland Organics, where she developed comprehensive educational content to help farmers and growers succeed with organic solutions.

## Contributions

During her time with Southland Organics, Isabella authored over 100 articles covering:

- **Poultry Health & Management** - Disease prevention, biosecurity, nutrition, and best practices for commercial and backyard poultry operations
- **Soil & Plant Health** - Humate applications, soil biology, and organic approaches to lawn and garden care
- **General Agriculture** - Farming practices, equipment tips, and industry insights

Her work continues to serve as a valuable resource for the agricultural community.
