## About This Topic

Probiotics aren't just for yogurt. In agriculture, beneficial bacteria play a critical role in animal health, nutrient absorption, and disease resistance. These episodes explore the science and practical application of probiotics across farming operations.

## Why It Matters

Probiotics in agriculture support:
- Improved gut health and feed conversion in poultry
- Reduced reliance on antibiotics
- Better nutrient cycling in soil
- Natural disease suppression
