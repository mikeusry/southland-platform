## Apple Cider Vinegar for Chickens: What It Does

Raw apple cider vinegar with the mother gives your flock naturally occurring organic acids, enzymes, and beneficial bacteria that support a healthy gut. A well-functioning gut means better nutrient absorption, steadier digestion, and birds that handle stress more easily. Mother Load is infused with garlic, which adds immune support on top of the digestive benefits — one simple addition to the water line.

## How Much Apple Cider Vinegar in Chicken Water

The standard rate is **1 oz of Mother Load per gallon of drinking water**. During high-stress periods — molting, summer heat, or a move — bump it up to 2 oz per gallon for three to five days to help the birds recover faster. Use a plastic waterer rather than galvanized metal, since ACV will corrode metal over time. Shake the container before adding, since settling is normal for raw, unfiltered ACV.

## A Natural Fit for Any Flock

Whether you keep a handful of backyard hens or run a commercial operation, Mother Load ACV fits your program without adding complexity. It runs through your existing water system, requires no withdrawal period, and works alongside your other poultry nutrition and health products.
