## Why Surface Sanitation Matters in Biosecurity

Cleaning removes the visible mess. Sanitation kills what you can't see. In poultry houses, food processing facilities, and commercial kitchens, the bacteria left behind after cleaning — E. coli, Salmonella, Listeria — are the ones that cause real damage. Alpet D2 closes that gap with a fast-acting, no-rinse formula that fits into any biosecurity or food safety protocol.

## Speed Without Compromise

Most EPA-registered sanitizers force a trade-off: effectiveness or convenience. Alpet D2 eliminates that choice. The alcohol-based formula evaporates in seconds, kills target organisms in under a minute, and requires no rinsing — even on food-contact surfaces. In a production environment where every minute counts, that speed translates directly to throughput and compliance.

## Built for the Real World

Alpet D2 works in the environments where sanitation matters most: between flock turnovers, at poultry house entry points, on processing equipment, and across commercial kitchen prep surfaces. Keep it at every entry point, on every cleaning cart, and in every truck cab. The best biosecurity protocol is the one that's easy enough to actually follow.
