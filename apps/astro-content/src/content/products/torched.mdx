## Why People Are Moving Away From Chemical Weed Killers

The conversation around herbicides has changed. Homeowners, schools, HOAs, and municipalities are looking for alternatives to glyphosate and synthetic chemicals — not because they don't work, but because of growing concerns about exposure, soil health, and environmental runoff.

Torched fills that gap. It delivers fast, visible weed kill using natural active ingredients that break down without leaving chemical residue in your soil or waterways.

## How It Works

Torched is a contact herbicide. When sprayed on weed foliage, it disrupts the plant's cell membranes, causing rapid desiccation. You'll see wilting within 1-2 hours and significant browning within 4 hours on a sunny day.

Because it's a contact killer (not systemic), it works on the plant tissue it touches. This makes it ideal for targeted spot treatment — driveways, sidewalk cracks, garden beds, fence lines, and patios — where you want precision without worrying about chemical drift or soil persistence.

## The Right Tool for the Right Job

Torched excels at:
- **Driveway and sidewalk cracks** — spray and forget
- **Garden bed edges** — clean up weeds without contaminating soil
- **Playground and pet areas** — safe after drying
- **Pre-plant bed prep** — burn down existing weeds before planting

For established lawn weeds growing in your turf, pair Torched with a soil health program (Humates + Genesis) that helps your grass outcompete weeds naturally over time.
