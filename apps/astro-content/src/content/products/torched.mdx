## Why People Are Moving Away From Chemical Weed Killers

The conversation around herbicides has changed. Homeowners, chicken keepers, regenerative farmers, schools, and HOAs are looking for alternatives to glyphosate and synthetic chemicals — not because the chemicals don't work, but because of growing concerns about exposure, soil health, pollinators, and runoff.

Torched fills that gap. It's made from clove and mint oil and delivers fast, visible weed kill without leaving chemical residue in your soil or waterways. We've been making it in the USA since 2017.

## How It Works

Torched is a contact herbicide. When sprayed on weed foliage, the plant oils disrupt the leaf's cell membranes, causing rapid desiccation. On a warm, sunny day you'll see wilting within hours.

Because it's a contact killer (not systemic), it works on the plant tissue it touches. That makes it ideal for targeted spot treatment — driveways, sidewalk cracks, garden beds, fence lines, and around coops and pastures — where you want precision without chemical drift or anything left behind in the soil.

## It Kills the Weed, Not the Ground

This is the most important thing to understand about Torched, and it's where people's expectations sometimes get crossed.

Glyphosate and other chemical herbicides keep a spot bare for months because they leave a residue that lingers in the soil and stops anything new from growing. That looks like "lasting" weed control. It's actually a sterilized patch of ground.

Torched does the opposite on purpose. It burns down the weed you spray and leaves nothing behind in the soil. So when new weeds appear a couple weeks later, those aren't the weeds you killed coming back to life — they're brand-new seeds sprouting in healthy, living soil. That's a living yard doing what living yards do. Spot-spray the new ones the same way.

We could make Torched "last longer" by adding soil-persistent chemistry. We won't — not in a product meant for gardens, pastures, and coops.

## The Right Tool for the Right Job

Torched excels at:

- **Driveway and sidewalk cracks** — spot-spray and move on
- **Garden bed edges** — clean up weeds without contaminating soil
- **Around coops, runs, barns, and fence lines** — the place chemical killers can't safely go
- **Pre-plant bed prep** — burn down existing weeds, then plant the same day with no residue to wait out

For established weeds growing inside your turf, pair Torched with a soil-health program (Humates + Genesis) so your grass outcompetes weeds naturally over time.
