## Sanitation That Travels With You

The best biosecurity protocol is the one your team actually follows. Spray bottles get left behind. Paper towels run out. But a canister of Alpet D2 wipes in the truck cab, at the house door, and on the cleaning cart means sanitation happens at the point of contact — not 10 minutes later when someone walks back to the supply room.

## Same Formula, Different Delivery

Alpet D2 Surface Sanitizing Wipes use the exact same EPA-registered formula as the spray. The lintless wipe material won't leave fibers on equipment, screens, or food-contact surfaces — a real advantage over generic cleaning wipes that shed and leave residue.

## Where to Stage Them

Put canisters everywhere people touch things: poultry house entry points, truck cabs, processing line stations, break room tables, office desks, and delivery vehicles. The per-wipe cost is negligible compared to the cost of a biosecurity failure or a food safety violation.
