## The Problem With Commodity Potting Soil

Walk into any big-box store and grab a bag of potting soil. Open it up. You'll find bark chunks, sticks, inconsistent texture, and material that compacts into a dense brick after a few waterings. That's what most indoor plants are living in — and it's why root rot is the number one killer of houseplants.

Perfect Pot is engineered, not assembled. Every ingredient has a job: peat moss holds moisture without waterlogging, perlite creates air channels that roots need to breathe, biochar holds nutrients in the root zone, and compost provides slow-release nutrition for months.

## Why Biochar Changes Everything

Most potting soils are biologically dead — they're just a medium for roots to sit in. Biochar changes that equation. Its porous structure provides surface area for beneficial soil microbes to colonize, creating a living ecosystem inside your pot.

Those microbes help break down organic matter into plant-available nutrients, suppress root pathogens, and improve the overall health of your growing environment. It's the same soil biology science used in professional agriculture, working inside your houseplant container.

## Pairs With Bloom Plant Food

Perfect Pot provides initial nutrition from compost for 2-3 months after potting. Once that's spent, pick up where the soil leaves off with Bloom liquid plant food. Together, they give your plants the growing medium and ongoing nutrition to thrive year-round.
