## The Missing Piece in Most Lawn Programs

Most lawn care programs focus on what goes on top: fertilizer, herbicide, fungicide. But the real engine of a healthy lawn is underground. Soil biology — the bacteria, fungi, and protozoa that cycle nutrients, suppress disease, and build soil structure — is what separates lawns that thrive from lawns that survive.

Genesis rebuilds that biology. It introduces 20+ species of beneficial microorganisms into depleted soil, restoring the natural processes that synthetic programs have suppressed.

## What Happens When Soil Biology Returns

When you rebuild microbial life, the cascading effects are visible:
- **Thatch breaks down naturally** — beneficial bacteria and fungi decompose dead organic matter without power raking
- **Roots go deeper** — mycorrhizal networks extend the effective root zone by up to 100x
- **Nutrients become available** — microbes convert locked-up minerals into plant-usable forms
- **Disease pressure drops** — diverse biology suppresses pathogenic fungi and bacteria through competitive exclusion

## The Foundation Product

Genesis is where organic lawn programs start. Pair it with Humates (humic acid that feeds and sustains the microbes) for a soil health foundation that reduces input costs over time while producing a stronger, more resilient lawn.

For turf professionals, Genesis + Humates is the biological base layer that makes everything else — fertility, pest management, cultural practices — work more efficiently.
