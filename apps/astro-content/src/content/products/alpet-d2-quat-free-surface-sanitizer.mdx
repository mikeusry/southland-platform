## Why Quat-Free Matters

Quaternary ammonium compounds have been the default sanitizer active ingredient for decades. But as food safety standards tighten and buyer requirements evolve, quats are becoming a liability in certain environments. Dairy processors, beverage manufacturers, pharmaceutical facilities, and laboratories increasingly require quat-free protocols because residue can contaminate product, interfere with quality testing, and fail third-party audits.

Alpet D2 Quat-Free delivers the same fast-acting, no-rinse surface sanitation as the original D2 — without the quaternary ammonium compounds that create compliance headaches.

## Same Speed, Zero Compromise

The alcohol-based formula evaporates in seconds, kills target organisms rapidly, and leaves no residue on food-contact or non-food-contact surfaces. No rinsing, no wiping, no waiting. In production environments where every minute counts, the Quat-Free D2 fits directly into existing sanitation protocols as a drop-in replacement.

## Built for Regulated Environments

Whether you're running a dairy line, a beverage fill station, or a poultry processing facility, Alpet D2 Quat-Free is EPA-registered for the surfaces that matter most. Keep it at workstations, entry points, and cleaning carts. The best sanitation protocol is the one your team actually follows — and the Quat-Free D2 makes compliance as simple as spray and walk away.
