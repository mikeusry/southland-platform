## Why Gut Health Matters in Swine Production

A pig's gut is the engine of its growth. When the digestive system is working well, feed converts efficiently, nutrients absorb properly, and the animal thrives. When it's not, you see it in slower gains, higher feed costs, and more health interventions — all of which come straight off your margin.

Apple cider vinegar with the mother provides naturally occurring organic acids, enzymes, and beneficial bacteria that support a healthy gut environment. It's not a miracle cure — it's a foundational tool that helps the digestive system do what it's supposed to do.

## Simple Water-Line Delivery

Mother Load ACV runs through your existing water system with no special equipment and no individual animal handling. Add it to the water line, and every pig on the system gets consistent, daily gut support. During high-stress periods like weaning or transport, increase the concentration for a few days to help the gut recover faster.

## A Natural Fit for Modern Operations

Whether you're running a commercial finishing operation or raising hogs on a small farm, Mother Load ACV fits into your program without adding complexity. It's compatible with conventional and natural protocols, requires no withdrawal period, and works alongside your existing nutrition and health programs.
