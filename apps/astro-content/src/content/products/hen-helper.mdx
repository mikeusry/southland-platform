## Why Gut Health Matters for Your Backyard Flock

Your chickens' gut is their engine. It's where feed becomes eggs, feathers, energy, and immune function. When the microbiome is out of balance — from stress, heat, dietary changes, or introducing new birds — everything downstream suffers: shell quality drops, feathers dull, laying becomes irregular, and birds get sick more easily.

Hen Helper introduces beneficial bacteria that crowd out harmful organisms and improve nutrient absorption. It's the same probiotic science that commercial farms rely on, sized and priced for backyard flocks.

## What You'll Notice

Most flock owners report changes within 2-3 weeks:
- **Stronger, more consistent shells** — fewer thin shells and breakage
- **Better feather condition** — especially noticeable after molts
- **More predictable laying** — fewer off days during prime production
- **Calmer, more active birds** — a healthy gut supports overall vitality

## Simple Enough for Every Morning

No syringes, no catching birds, no complicated mixing. Add one tablespoon to each gallon of drinking water and refill as normal. Every hen that drinks gets her dose. It's the easiest thing you can add to your flock's routine.
