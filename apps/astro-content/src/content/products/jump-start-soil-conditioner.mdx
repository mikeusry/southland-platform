## The Foundation Nobody Thinks About

Most lawn and garden problems start underground. Compacted clay, depleted biology, locked-up nutrients — these are soil problems masquerading as grass problems or plant problems. You can throw fertilizer at them all season and see marginal improvement because the soil can't process what you're giving it.

Jump Start addresses the root cause. Its organic acid complex stimulates microbial activity, chelates locked-up minerals, and improves soil structure from the first application. It's the step that makes everything else you do work better.

## New Construction's Dirty Secret

If your home was built in the last 20 years, your yard is probably growing on subsoil. Builders strip the topsoil, compact what's left with heavy equipment, then spread an inch of fill and lay sod on top. The grass looks great for a few weeks, then slowly declines because there's nothing underneath supporting it.

Jump Start is how you rebuild what the builder took. Apply it before or after sod installation to start reconditioning the soil. Follow with Genesis to introduce the biology that turns dead dirt into living soil.

## Pairs With Your Soil Health Program

Jump Start works best as the first step in a soil restoration sequence. Apply Jump Start to condition the soil, follow with Genesis to introduce biology, and maintain with Humates to feed and sustain the microbial population. Together, they rebuild the underground engine that makes lawns, gardens, and crops thrive without increasing chemical inputs.
