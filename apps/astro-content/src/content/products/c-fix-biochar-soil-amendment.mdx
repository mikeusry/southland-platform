## Why Biochar Is Different From Everything Else You Add to Soil

Compost breaks down. Fertilizer washes out. Biochar stays. When you work C-Fix into your soil, you're making a permanent structural improvement. The porous carbon matrix creates millions of tiny pores that hold water, trap nutrients, and provide habitat for the beneficial microorganisms that drive soil health. It's not an input you reapply every season — it's an upgrade that compounds over time.

## The Humic Acid Advantage

Most biochar products require a "charging" period where the raw biochar absorbs nutrients from the soil before it starts giving them back. C-Fix skips that step. By infusing the biochar with humic acid during production, every granule arrives ready to work — chelating nutrients, feeding microbes, and improving nutrient availability from day one.

## Where to Use It

C-Fix works in any soil, in any setting. Mix it into raised beds before planting. Top-dress your lawn during renovation. Blend it into container potting mix. Work it into clay soil to improve drainage, or into sandy soil to improve retention. Wherever soil needs help, biochar provides the permanent foundation that makes every other input work better.
