## Why Vitamins Matter for Backyard Flocks

Feed is the foundation, but it's not the whole picture. Commercial layer feed is formulated for baseline nutrition under ideal conditions. Your backyard flock doesn't live in ideal conditions — they deal with Georgia summers, cold snaps, molting stress, new flock members, and the occasional predator scare.

Catalyst Liquid fills the gaps that feed can't cover. Vitamins A, D, E, and K support immune function, egg production, feather quality, and bone health. Electrolytes help birds manage heat stress and recover from illness faster.

## Liquid Beats Powder Every Time

If you've tried powdered vitamin supplements, you already know the frustration. They clump at the bottom of the waterer, don't dissolve evenly, and half your flock drinks before the other half gets any real dose.

Catalyst Liquid mixes instantly and stays suspended in water. Every bird that drinks gets a consistent dose. No stirring, no scooping, no waste sitting at the bottom of the bowl.
