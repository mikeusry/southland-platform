## Why Gut Health Matters in Commercial Poultry

The broiler gut is where profit is made or lost. A healthy microbiome means better nutrient absorption, stronger immune response, and fewer losses to enteric disease. Big Ole Bird establishes beneficial bacteria from day one, giving your birds a competitive advantage before pathogenic bacteria can colonize.

Unlike antibiotics that kill indiscriminately, probiotics work by competitive exclusion — beneficial bacteria occupy binding sites in the gut lining, leaving no room for harmful organisms like *E. coli*, *Clostridium*, and *Salmonella*.

## The Science Behind the Settlement Check

In a 2019 University of Georgia field trial, houses treated with Big Ole Bird showed:
- **6.2% reduction in mortality** vs. untreated control houses
- **0.04-point FCR improvement** (translating to ~$600-1,200 savings per 20,000-bird house per flock)
- **Reduced veterinary intervention** for enteric issues

These aren't lab results — they're from commercial broiler houses under real production conditions.

## Built for the Way You Farm

Big Ole Bird is designed for commercial-scale operations. It runs through your existing water-line system with zero additional labor. The shelf-stable formula doesn't need refrigeration, and it's compatible with standard water treatment up to 3 ppm chlorine.

Whether you're running conventional or NAE programs, Big Ole Bird fits. It's USDA Organic compliant with no withdrawal period, making it one of the few performance tools available to certified organic producers.
