## Why Monthly Matters More Than Once-a-Year

Septic systems are biological engines. The bacteria inside your tank are supposed to break down everything that goes in — solids, grease, paper, soap. But modern households overwhelm them. Antibacterial soap, bleach-based cleaners, and high-volume usage kill off the very bacteria your system needs to function.

A single treatment can't fix that. Monthly biological replenishment keeps the bacterial population high enough to actually process what your household produces. It's the difference between a system that slowly deteriorates and one that maintains itself.

## The Math Is Simple

A pump-out costs $300-500. A drain field replacement costs $5,000-15,000. Pour the PORT costs roughly $20 a month. If it extends your pump-out interval by even one cycle, it's paid for itself several times over. If it prevents a drain field failure, it's the best insurance policy your home has.

The subscription exists because consistency is everything with septic biology. You can't remember to treat your tank every month for years — nobody can. We ship it, you pour it in the toilet before bed, flush twice, done. The bacteria do the rest while you sleep.
