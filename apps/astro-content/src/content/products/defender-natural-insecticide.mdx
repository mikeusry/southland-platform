## Why Natural Pest Control Matters

The conventional approach to pest control is simple: kill everything with chemicals and deal with the consequences later. But those consequences add up — toxic residue on countertops, dead pollinators in the garden, kids and pets banished from treated areas for hours.

Defender takes a different approach. Natural active ingredients provide effective knockdown on contact pests while breaking down without leaving harmful residue behind. You get pest control that works in the spaces where your family actually lives.

## One Product, Every Space

Most households buy separate products for indoor bugs, outdoor pests, and garden insects. Defender handles all three. Spray baseboards for ants, treat garden beds for aphids, hit the barn for flies — same bottle, same safety profile, no switching between chemical formulations.

For farms and homesteads where animals, children, and gardens share the same ground, that simplicity matters. One product you trust in every corner of your property.
