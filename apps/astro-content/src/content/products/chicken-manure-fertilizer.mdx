## Why Organic Fertilizer Outperforms Synthetic Over Time

Synthetic fertilizers feed the plant. Organic fertilizers feed the soil — and the soil feeds the plant. That distinction matters more than most people realize. Every application of Chicken Manure Fertilizer adds organic matter that beneficial soil microbes use as food. Those microbes improve soil structure, cycle nutrients, and suppress disease. Over two to three seasons, your soil becomes a self-sustaining ecosystem that needs less input, not more.

## The 4-3-2 Advantage

Not all fertilizers need to be high-nitrogen bombs. The balanced 4-3-2 NPK ratio delivers nitrogen for green growth, phosphorus for root development, and potassium for stress tolerance — without the excess that causes fertilizer burn, rapid growth spurts, or nutrient runoff. The slow-release formula breaks down gradually as soil microbes process the organic material, feeding your lawn and garden steadily over weeks instead of days.
