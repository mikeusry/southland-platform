## Why Vitamins Matter During Stress

A bird's vitamin reserves deplete rapidly under stress. Heat, vaccination, transport, disease challenge — each event draws down the reserves that fuel immune response, growth, and recovery. Without supplementation, birds recover slower, eat less, grow less, and die more.

Catalyst delivers a concentrated dose of vitamins A, D3, E, and K plus electrolytes through the water line. It's not a replacement for good management — it's an insurance policy that costs a penny per bird and protects your settlement check when conditions get tough.

## The Heat Stress Equation

Summer flocks consistently underperform winter flocks. The math is simple: above 85°F, birds reduce feed intake by 2-5%. They pant instead of eat. They burn calories on thermoregulation instead of growth. Mortality spikes.

Catalyst doesn't change the temperature. It gives birds the metabolic resources to cope with it — better hydration, stronger immune function, and faster recovery when the heat breaks. Growers using Catalyst during summer flocks report 3-5% less heat-related mortality.

## Pairs With Your Probiotic Program

Catalyst and Big Ole Bird are complementary tools. Probiotics build the gut foundation daily. Vitamins provide targeted support during acute stress events. Together, they create a resilient bird from placement through processing.
