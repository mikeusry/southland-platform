## About Dr. Mitchell

Dr. Sarah Mitchell's work has been instrumental in advancing our understanding of poultry gut health. Her research has shown that investing in gut health early can reduce mortality rates and improve feed conversion in commercial operations.

## Research Focus

- Gut microbiome development in young poultry
- Probiotic effectiveness in commercial settings
- Antibiotic alternatives for poultry health
- Nutrition's role in disease resistance

## Publications

Dr. Mitchell has published extensively on poultry nutrition and health. Her most cited work includes studies on:

- Early-life microbiome development
- Probiotic strains effective in poultry
- The gut-immune connection in birds
