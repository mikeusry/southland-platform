## About Anna

Anna Watson Munzenmaier is a UGA cheerleader turned strength coach at D1 Training Athens, widely known as "the world's strongest cheerleader." A co-founder of JockShock, she shapes how training programs land inside real facilities and shares the strength-coach perspective on athlete performance, recovery, and what actually works in the gym.
