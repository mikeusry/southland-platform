## Optimizing Breeder Performance With Natural Solutions

Breeder operations face unique challenges that directly impact your bottom line. ## Probiotics and Gut Health for Breeder Hens

Healthy gut bacteria play a crucial role in breeder hen performance. Beneficial microbes help hens digest feed more completely, support immune function, and maintain consistent egg production throughout the lay cycle. Your house environment directly affects egg quality and hatchability. Poor litter conditions create ammonia buildup that stresses birds and reduces performance. Healthy litter means healthier birds, better air quality, and improved working conditions for your staff.
