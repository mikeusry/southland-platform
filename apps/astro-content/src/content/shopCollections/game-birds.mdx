## The Largest Quail Producer in the Country Starts With a Probiotic

When we wanted to talk game birds, we went to John Tuck of **Tuck Farms — the world's largest bobwhite quail hatchery,** producing toward 1.4 million chicks a year. His advice for quail growers is the foundation of everything we make: _"a good probiotic system is a good start."_ He runs [Big Ole Bird](/products/big-ole-bird) for probiotics and [Litter Life](/products/litter-life) for litter condition, and keeps proven products on hand so he can act the day a problem shows up.

That last part matters most with game birds. Quail need minimal interaction — John may see his birds only 15-20 minutes a week — so by the time you spot a problem like ulcerative enteritis, you don't have time to wait on a vet prescription. His whole approach, on video, is to stay ahead of it with a strong baseline program rather than scramble to react.

## Natural Supplements for Game Bird Operations

Your game bird operation faces unique challenges. Whether you're raising quail, pheasant, chukar, or guinea fowl, you need solutions that work with your birds' natural biology—not against it.

The key is strengthening beneficial bacteria populations in both your birds' digestive systems and their environment. When good bacteria dominate, pathogenic organisms can't establish strongholds that lead to poor performance and mortality.

## Probiotics and Gut Health for Quail, Pheasant, and Game Birds

Game birds have sensitive digestive systems that respond well to targeted probiotic support. Our [Poultry Probiotic](/products/poultry-probiotic) contains hearty Bacillus species that survive the journey to the gut and establish beneficial colonies.

Unlike typical poultry probiotics that only feature Lactobacillus acidophilus, our formulation includes diverse bacterial strains that work together to improve gut integrity and nutrient absorption. This translates to better feed conversion and stronger birds throughout grow-out.

## Vitamins and Nutritional Support for Game Bird Grow-Out

Stress periods demand additional nutritional support. Our [Catalyst Poultry Vitamin](/products/catalyst-poultry-vitamin) and [Liquid Catalyst](/products/liquid-catalyst-poultry-vitamin) provide targeted nutrition when your birds face challenges like weather changes, transport, or housing transitions.

## Game Bird House Litter and Ammonia Management

Ammonia control starts with litter management. Your game bird houses are biological battlefields where good and bad bacteria compete for dominance. Disinfectants wipe out both, creating opportunities for harmful organisms to take over.

Our [Poultry Litter Amendment](/products/poultry-litter-amendment) introduces beneficial microbial populations that outcompete ammonia-producing bacteria naturally. This biological approach reduces ammonia levels while maintaining the healthy bacteria your birds need.

## Natural Insect Control for Game Bird Facilities

Insect pressure affects bird comfort and performance. Our [Natural Mite Control](/products/desecticide) uses essential oils and organic compounds to manage mites and other insects without synthetic pesticides.

This approach protects your birds while preserving beneficial insects that contribute to a balanced facility ecosystem. The result is effective pest control that doesn't disrupt the natural defenses you're working to strengthen.
