## The Proof: Two Third-Party Studies, Not Marketing Claims

We got tired of "you saved our farm" reviews being the only evidence — amazing to hear, but we wanted to know the science was real. So we put Big Ole Bird through two third-party performance studies with poultry-science programs growers trust: the **University of Georgia College of Veterinary Medicine** and the **Southern Poultry Research Group.**

- **2016 trial** (Cobb 500 males, challenged with necrotic enteritis / _Clostridium perfringens_): birds given Big Ole Bird showed a **16-point feed conversion improvement** and **6.2% lower mortality** than the challenged birds that didn't get it.
- **2018 trial** (Ross 708, in an SPRG facility built to mimic commercial broiler conditions): the treated birds carried **nearly an extra half-pound** at day 38 and a **7-point feed conversion improvement** — and showed measurably healthier bursas, the organ that drives a bird's antibody defenses.

That's real research, with real institutions, on the exact challenges — necrotic enteritis, mortality, feed conversion — you fight in your own houses.
