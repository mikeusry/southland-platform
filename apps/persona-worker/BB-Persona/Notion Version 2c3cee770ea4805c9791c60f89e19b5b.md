## **HOW TO SPEAK TO BROILER BILL**

### **Language & Terminology**

**Use Industry-Specific Terms:**

- "Placement" not "putting chicks in"
- "Mortality" not "death rate"
- "FCR" (Feed Conversion Ratio) not "feed efficiency"
- "Livability" not "survival rate"
- "Grow-out" not "raising period"
- "House ranking" not "performance comparison"
- "Settlement" not "payment"

**Numbers That Matter to Him:**

- **Mortality percentage** (should be <3%)
- **FCR ratios** (1.75-1.85 is excellent)
- **Average daily gain** (grams per day)
- **Settlement ranking** (top 5 vs. bottom 5)
- **Condemnation rates**
- **Cost per pound produced**

### **Message Framing**

**Lead With Performance Metrics:** ❌ "Our products help chickens" ✅ "Improved FCR by 0.08 points and reduced mortality to 2.1% in University of Georgia trial"

**Use Peer Testimonials:** ❌ "Customers love our products" ✅ "Jody S., 4-house operation in Alabama: 'I have the best mortality of any growers around. Farmers are fighting for my litter.'"

**Frame ROI Clearly:** ❌ "Save money with our products" ✅ "At $0.05 per bird improvement, pays for itself with 500 birds per house"
