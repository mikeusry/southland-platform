### 14. Five Objections + Answers

**Q: I already have a slurry recipe that works. Why change?**
A: You're not changing your recipe — you're adding to it. Genesis and Omega go into your existing tank with your existing mulch and fertilizer. Same equipment, same process. The difference is what happens in the soil after you spray. If your current recipe delivers consistent day-6 breakthrough and zero callbacks, don't change a thing. If not, test it on one job.

**Q: How does this mix with my existing mulch and fertilizer?**
A: Add it before water so it coats seeds and mulch in the agitator. 1 gallon of Omega per 500 gallons of slurry. Genesis at 10-12 oz per 1,000 sq ft. Compatible with all standard hydroseeding mulches and fertilizer programs. No pH issues, no clumping, no equipment modifications.

**Q: 155% germination improvement sounds too good. Is that real?**
A: That's from a third-party study by TRI Environmental — not our marketing department. They tested Genesis-treated seeds against untreated control.
