The purpose of this meeting is not video production.  The purpose is to broaden our process for all communications. Currently we have a loose process.  We are going to form a systematic and tactical approaches to our communication strategy so that we can move forward with the most efficiency and confidence.

The Entire Process is broken up into 3 systems

- Strategy
    - Research. Apply the “templates”
- Design
    - Take the data and design the talking points and “levers” to pull
- Growth
    - Implement funnels, advertising, media etc.

You start with your customer hero.  What do they need?  What is the problem or pain point that they must overcome?

- We have to identify the who (Personas)
- We have to identify the what (Pains, JTBD)
- We have to identify the when (Stages)

Then we pull the levers to change the universe for the hero.  The levers are the action items for us to use to shape the journey.
